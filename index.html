Welcome, Herpetology Enthusiasts!

The TDAC is dedicated to the Moloch horridus, an incredible lizard found in the arid scrublands of Central Australia. Our goal is to spread awareness about their unique survival mechanisms and desert ecology.

Hygroscopic Skin

The Thorny Devil can drink through its feet! Capillary action pulls water from damp sand into its mouth.

The "False" Head

They have a knob on their neck they use as a decoy to confuse predators during an attack.

Dietary Specialist

A single lizard can consume between 600 to 2,500 ants in a single meal.

Thermal Regulation

They change color to regulate body temperature, appearing darker in the cool mornings.
